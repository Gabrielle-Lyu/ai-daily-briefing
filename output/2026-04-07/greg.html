◆ OCI Implication of the Day
The Uber-AWS Trainium deal is the sharpest competitive signal of the day: a top-tier enterprise is deepening chip-level lock-in with AWS for AI inference workloads, explicitly at Oracle's expense. OCI must accelerate its differentiated AI infrastructure narrative — specifically the Nvidia Supercluster density advantage and the sovereign/regulated-cloud angle — before similar decisions solidify at other large enterprise accounts. The absence of OCI from Anthropic's Mythos cybersecurity launch, combined with Oracle's massive Michigan data center announcement, creates a strategic asymmetry: OCI is winning the infrastructure capacity race on paper but losing the AI model partnership and workload conversion battles that determine long-term platform stickiness.
